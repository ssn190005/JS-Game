Treasure Hunt!

Instructions to Play!

You must capture 100 points to release level badge.

Across the level you will find coins spread out for taking. Each coin fetches you 10 points.

Besides, you would also come across certain stars. Grabbing a star would either give you a chance to score 10 points or else cost you your game. Each star demands that you answer a simple question with a 'Yes' or 'No' to proceed further.

Controls --- Use spacebar to jump and your arrow keys to navigate

Note - Mobile users, please stay tuned for support!

Happy Gaming!
