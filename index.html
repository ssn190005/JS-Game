Treasure Hunt!

Instructions to Play!

1. There is a treasure out there. Collect coins and stars to win this hunt.

2.If you are eager to play, use 'SPACEBAR' to jump and 'Arrow keys' to navigate. Go Play!

3.If you want to know more, read on.

4.Collecting each coin fetches you 10 points.

5.You must score 100 points to qualify for completion of a level.

6.Once you score 100 points the level badge will be released.

7.Collect the level badge to move to the next level.

8.Across each level you will find certain special items also, each of which may have different effects.

9.Level#1 will have Stars spread out for you. Each star fetches a question that you must answer in Yes or No.

10.If you give the right answer, you get 10 points, else your game would be over.

11.Watch out for other items and their details in subsequent levels

Note - Mobile users, please stay tuned for support!

Happy Gaming!
